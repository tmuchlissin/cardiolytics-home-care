Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Cardiolytics Home Care features and services.

How does the Blood Pressure Monitor work?

The Blood Pressure Monitor feature allows you to track your blood pressure in real-time using a compatible device. The data is displayed in an easy-to-read integrated dashboard, helping you monitor heart health trends and share results with your doctor.

What is Cardiovascular Predict, and how accurate are its predictions?

Cardiovascular Predict uses advanced AI models to analyze your health data and predict cardiovascular risks. The model is trained with trusted medical data to provide accurate results and personalized recommendations to prevent heart issues.

How does Cardiobot help me understand my heart health?

Cardiobot is an AI-powered assistant that provides quick and reliable answers to your questions about heart health. Based on documents from medical institutions, Cardiobot explains information in easy-to-understand language whenever you need it.

Is my health data secure on the Cardiolytics platform?

We use high-level encryption and comply with international privacy standards to protect your health data. All information is securely stored and accessible only to you and the medical professionals you authorize.

Do I need special equipment to use these services?

For the Blood Pressure Monitor feature, you need a blood pressure measuring device compatible with our app. Cardiovascular Predict and Cardiobot can be used directly through the website or app without additional equipment.
