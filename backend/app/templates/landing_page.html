FAQ

Frequently asked questions about our services.

How do I get started with Cardiolytics services?

You can sign up via our mobile app or website. Once registered, you'll be asked to provide initial health data and then start monitoring your heart condition using our supported devices.

Is this service suitable for all ages?

Cardiolytics services are designed for users of all ages, especially for those who wish to monitor their heart health regularly.

What about the cost of doctor consultations?

Consultation fees vary depending on your subscription plan. Detailed pricing and payment methods are available on our app and website.

How secure is my health data?

We use state-of-the-art encryption and adhere to strict privacy protocols to ensure your health data remains secure and confidential.

Can I use the service internationally?

Yes, Cardiolytics is accessible worldwide, allowing you to monitor your heart health no matter where you are.
